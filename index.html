The importance of fluency skills

You have probably heard the old quote, “Knowledge is Power,” That’s truer than you can ever imagine. Few people understand how important knowledge can be. Knowledge is what allows us to drive cars instead of ride horses, it is what helps us survive far longer than we should, and knowledge is even what prevents us from making the same mistakes we made in the past. But all of this comes down to one thing, the ability to understand and interpret information, without fluency skills there is no way we can fully understand information and solve problems presented to us. Essentially, we need information fluency in order to be able to find solutions to the problems presented. In other words, solution fluency is built on information fluency.

Information fluency

Information Fluency is the “ability to unconsciously and intuitively interpret information in all forms and formats in order to extract the essential knowledge, perceive its meaning and significance, and use it to complete real-world tasks” (Churches, Jukes & Crockett, 2011: 33).

According to Crockett( 2015,8) There is a lot of information on the internet. With new information being added every second, and because of this information gets obsolete far quicker than we can keep up making it hard to find relevant with the knowledge built on those facts becoming less durable. Due to the rapid increase of information being added on the internet unless information also increases. information fluency as a process will help to determine the credibility, authenticity, and veracity of the information presented.
